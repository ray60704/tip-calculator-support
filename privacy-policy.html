Privacy Policy for Happy Tip

Last updated: July 2025

Overview

Happy Tip is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle any information when you use our tip calculator app.

Information We Collect

Happy Tip does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The app operates entirely on your device and does not require any personal data to function.

Data Storage

All calculations and any preferences you set within the app are stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to this information.

Third-Party Services

Happy Tip does not integrate with any third-party services that collect personal information.

Analytics

We do not use any analytics services or tracking tools within the Happy Tip app.

Children's Privacy

Happy Tip does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
